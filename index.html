About Me

As touched upon at the top of the page, I am a 27 year old student at Staffordshire University, studying Computer Games Programming.

After 5 years as a carer I decided to pursue my passion for video games and re-enter higher education with the aim of entering the ever-growing games industry as a gameplay programmer.

So far in my studies I have primarily programmed in C++, with every day being a valuable learning experience.

Brief summaries of my work can be seen in the "Portfolio" section further down the page, including projects that are currently in progress.

Outside of my studies and programming, I enjoy playing video games, quizzing, escape rooms and playing the guitar.

Future Objectives

In my studies I shall soon begin a module on Artificial Intelligence in Games. This is a keen interest that I intend to dive into to augment my primary aim of becoming a gameplay programmer in the games industry.

Though not something that we use as part of the course, I am keen to continue learning how to use Unreal Engine 4 in order to create higher quality portfolio pieces to showcase on this site.

I am currently in the process of looking for a programming placement for the sandwich year of my degree.

Outside of my studies I intend to build on the foundations covered in previous work, creating a variety of small projects, with the aim of honing my skills and striving to further demonstrate and challenge my programming abilities.
